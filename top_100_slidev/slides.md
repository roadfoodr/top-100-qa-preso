<h2 class="slide-title">Extracting & Classifying Questions</h2>

<div class="slide-grid">
<div class="slide-text-col">

- **Input:** Discord logs from **four cohorts** (~1,300 threads)
- **Extracted first post** from each thread (building on work by Pastor Soto)
- **Filtered out** instructor and BiR posts
- **LLM classified** each post: genuine question? Content / Logistics / Other?
- **Result:** ~750 content questions

<h2 class="slide-title">Consolidating into Canonical Questions</h2>

<div class="slide-grid">
<div class="slide-text-col">

- **Many questions repeated** across cohorts — needed to consolidate
- **Key decision: granularity/specificity**
  - Too broad: different questions merged
  - Too narrow: similar questions left separate
  - e.g., "What vector DB?" and "How to configure ChromaDB?" — superficially similar topic, different intent, so don't merge
- **Grouped by intent** first, topic second
- **Three parallel approaches**, then merged into final list
- **Result:** ~100 canonical questions, ranked by popularity

<h2 class="slide-title">Automated Top 100</h2>

<div class="slide-grid">
<div class="slide-text-col">

- **Answers generated by Claude Code** via custom skill
- **Style guide** extracted from the top-ten document:
  - 150-250 words per answer
  - Lead with an **opinionated thesis**, follow with practical advice
  - **Conversational-expert** tone — no academic or marketing language
- **Curated knowledge base** for the skill to search:
  - Discord transcripts, course reader chapters
  - Earlier automated answers, workshop summaries & transcripts
  - Top-ten Q&A document
- **Skill workflow:** read questions -> search sources -> draft answers -> write output
